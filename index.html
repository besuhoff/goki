In this game called Goki you need to guess an ordered combination of 4 coloured chips. The possible colours are blue, green, yellow, orange, and red. Place an assumed combination of chips by clicking holes in a larger box of an available row. Choose chip colour by iterating through 5 available colours - left mouse button goes forward while right mouse click goes backwards.

Once ready with the assumption, hit "Respond!" button. I will respond with placing an unordered set of black and white chips into a smaller box. Each black chip means one of your coloured chips' position and colour matches exactly the colour and the position of a chip in my combination. Each white chip means that you guessed the colour of one of my chips but placed it in a different position. Empty holes mean that all the rest of the chips in my combination don't match the colour of any of yours.

Think carefully! If you fill up the whole board before guessing the combination, you lose.
