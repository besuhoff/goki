In this game called Goki you need to guess an ordered combination of 4 colored chips. The possible colors are blue, green, yellow, orange, and red. Place an assumed combination of chips by clicking holes in a larger box of an available row. Choose chip color by iterating through 5 available colors - left mouse button goes forward while right mouse click goes backwards.

Once ready with the assumption, hit "Respond!" button. I will respond with placing few black and white chips into a smaller hole in response. Each black chip means one of your chips' position and color matches exactly the color and the position of a chip in my combination. Each white chip means that you guessed the color of one of my chips but placed it in a different position. Empty holes mean that all the rest of the chips in my combination don't match the color of any of yours.

Think carefully! If you fill up the whole board before guessing the combination, you lose.
